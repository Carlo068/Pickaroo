# Pickaroo

Pickaroo is a platform that allows small businesses to easily offer pickup
services to their customers without building complex delivery infrastructure.

Customers can place orders online and schedule a pickup time, while businesses
manage orders and pickup slots through a simple dashboard.

## Features

- 🛒 Shop order pickup scheduling
- ⏰ Time slot management
- 📦 Order tracking
- 👤 Customer accounts
- 🏪 Business dashboard
- 🔔 Notifications for pickup readiness

## How It Works

1. A shop registers on the platform.
2. The shop adds products or services.
3. Customers place an order online.
4. Customers select a pickup time.
5. The shop prepares the order.
6. The customer receives a notification when the order is ready.
7. The customer picks up the order.

## Why Pickaroo?

Small businesses often struggle to implement pickup systems.
Pickaroo provides a simple, plug-and-play solution that allows any shop to
offer pickup services without building their own infrastructure.
